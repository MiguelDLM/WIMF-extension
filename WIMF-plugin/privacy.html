Privacy Policy for "Where is my fossil?" Extension

Last updated: February 2024

Information Collection

The "Where is my fossil?" extension does not collect any personal information. When you select text and use the context menu option, the selected text is sent only to the Where is my fossil? website to perform the search.

Data Storage

The extension stores only your preferences (window size) locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This information never leaves your browser.

Third-Party Services

The extension opens the Where is my fossil? website (https://migueldlm.github.io/Where-is-my-fossil/), which may have its own privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
